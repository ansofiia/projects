Pirates of the Caribbean

The Pirates of the Caribbean series is a series of films based on the original Disneyland attraction Pirates of the Caribbean. Distributed by Walt Disney Pictures and produced by Jerry Bruckheimer, the films were directed by Gore Verbinski, Rob Marshall, and Joachim Rønning and Espen Sandberg. They were most notably written by Ted Elliott and Terry Rossio; other writers include Stuart Beattie, Jay Wolpert, and Jeff Nathanson. Set in a fantasy world with a fictional historical setting, the films follow the adventures of the infamous Captain Jack Sparrow, joined by Captain Hector Barbossa and Joshamee Gibbs, as well as Elizabeth Swann, and Will Turner.
